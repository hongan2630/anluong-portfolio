Ann Luong

Artist | Installation | Digital Media

Bio

Ann Luong is a Vietnamese artist currently based in San Jose, California. She studies Digital Media Art at San Jose State University. Her work explores installation art, sculpture, and visual storytelling inspired by culture and memory.

Artist Statement

My work explores identity, memory, and cultural heritage. Through installation and sculptural forms, I examine how personal stories and collective histories shape our understanding of space and belonging.
